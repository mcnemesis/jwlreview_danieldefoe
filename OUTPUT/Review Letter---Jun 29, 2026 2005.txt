DETAILED SYNOPSIS:

Joseph Willrich Lutalo’s monograph presents dated reading notes and selected excerpts made between January 2025 and May 2026. Each entry pairs Defoe’s text with analytic and personal commentary, uncovering overlooked details and showing how sustained, reflective reading deepens our understanding of this classic for both scholars and general readers. The review is an original contribution to modern literature, surfacing parallels between Lutalo's existing novels, novellas, research, and the authoritatively recognized first modern English novel; Robinson Crusoe (1719).
